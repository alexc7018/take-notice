Take Notice A WordPress Plugin

Display special notices anywhere on your WordPress site.

View the Plugin on WordPress.org plugins/take-notice

  • Download .zip
  • Download .tar.gz
  • View On GitHub

Take Notice lets you add notices to any post or page on your WordPress site.

It even supports custom post types and taxonomies.

Each notice is managed seperately and can be customized to your heart's content.
